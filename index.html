About Me

I am currently working as a Chemist for the San Francisco Public Utilities Commission. I analyze finished drinking water to ensure all organic compounds of interest are below the maximum contamination limit. Luckily the water sources used for the city of San Francisco are from a very clean source, so very minimal modification is required to maintain acceptable qualities. I am interested in data analysis after realizing how much data I currently produce at my current occupation.

I grew up in northern California and have spend a fair amount of time in western Massachusetts. I attended Cal Poly in San Luis Obispo where gained a B.S. in Soil Science, focusing on Environmental Management. My interests include motorsports, cooking, hiking and learning to code.
